How I work

Senior product designer with 5+ years in complex, data-heavy software. Currently leading design at A-LIGN.

For the past two years I've led design for A-LIGN's compliance and audit platform, used by tens of thousands of auditors and clients, where the work is making dense, high-stakes information clear enough to act on.

I work end to end, from research and design systems through to interactive prototypes I build in Figma and Claude Code, so ideas get tested as real, working flows instead of static mockups.

I've designed AI-assisted features, analytics dashboards, and complex audit workflows. Simplifying data-heavy processes into experiences that work for expert and non-expert users alike.

I partner cross-functionally with product, engineering, and marketing, from concept through QA. I also bring a strong foundation in motion design and illustration that adds polish and clarity to every interaction.
