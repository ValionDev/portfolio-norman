Visual Pieces

A collection of illustration and graphic design work, exploring character design, digital painting, and visual storytelling across personal and professional projects.

About This Work

Beyond product design, I have a deep background in illustration and visual design. These pieces represent the creative side of my practice, work that sharpens my eye for composition, color, and storytelling, skills that directly inform my product design decisions.

Space Delivery

A digital illustration exploring sci-fi character and environment design. The piece focuses on atmospheric lighting, depth, and narrative composition, telling a story through a single frame. Created using Procreate and Illustrator.

Catch The Bucks!

Character and graphic design for a playful, game-inspired concept. The work explores bold color palettes, dynamic poses, and a graphic style that bridges illustration and UI design. The clean shapes and strong silhouettes reflect the same principles I apply to icon and component design in product work.

Can We Build A Spaceship?

A whimsical illustration blending technical and imaginative elements. This piece plays with the tension between engineering precision and childlike wonder, detailed mechanical elements rendered in a warm, approachable style. Created as an exercise in balancing complexity with visual clarity.
